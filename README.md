## Troubleshooting Approach

My support approach follows a consistent structure:

1. Define the issue clearly  
2. Understand the business impact  
3. Classify the problem type  
4. Reproduce and validate the issue  
5. Investigate relevant data, requests, logs, or workflows  
6. Form a likely root-cause hypothesis  
7. Identify next steps, workaround, or escalation path  

This approach is reflected throughout the case studies in this repository.
